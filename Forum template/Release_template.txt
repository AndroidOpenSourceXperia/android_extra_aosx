[B]How to install:[/B]
[LIST=1]
[*] Download the ROM
[*] Extract boot.img and flash it
[*] Copy the ROM to your SD Card
[*] Boot into recovery
[*] Wipe data, cache, dalvik cache, system (Wipe -> Advanced Wipe)
[*] Go to Mount and uncheck system
[*] Install the ROM
[*] Reboot
[/LIST]
