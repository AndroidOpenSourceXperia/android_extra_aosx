[COLOR="Lime"][SIZE="4"][B]Installation Instructions:[/B][/SIZE][/COLOR]
=================
- Download and copy ROM zip to your SDCard
- Flash the kernel
- Boot into recovery
- Wipe data, cache, dalvik cache and system.
- Flash ROM zip.
- Reboot
